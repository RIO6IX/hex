Welcome to CorteX CTF

CorteX CTF is an immersive cybersecurity platform designed to help you learn and practice ethical hacking skills through hands-on challenges. Our capture-the-flag competitions offer a wide range of difficulty levels, making it suitable for both beginners and seasoned security enthusiasts. Whether you're diving into Forensics, Reverse Engineering, Cryptography, or Binary Exploitation, CorteX CTF provides a safe, legal environment to sharpen your skills and push your limits in the world of cybersecurity.
